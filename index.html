How to Use Your Car Cleaning Kit?

Follow these simple steps for a professional-level clean at home:

  1. Rinse your car thoroughly to remove loose dirt
  2. Apply shampoo with a wash mitt from top to bottom
  3. Rinse and dry using quality microfiber towels
  4. Apply wax or sealant to protect your paint and enhance shine

With the right kit and proper technique, your car will look showroom-ready every time.
