The 4-Step Professional Protocol

Follow these simple steps to get pure neat look at home:

  1. Decontaminate: Start with a deep rinse to remove abrasive grit and prevent swirl marks.
  2. Agitate & Pure: Use our premium wash mitts and shampoo to lift dirt safely from the surface.
  3. Micro-Dry: Utilize ultra-absorbent towels to eliminate water spots for a streak-free shine.
  4. Seal & Shield: Apply a layer of high-performance wax or sealant to lock in the glow and repel water.

With the right kit and proper technique, your car will look showroom-ready every time.
